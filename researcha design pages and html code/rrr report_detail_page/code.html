Digital Commerce 2024: Global Trends

A comprehensive analysis of the evolving retail landscape, digital acceleration, and consumer behavior shifts across 45 markets.

Executive Summary

As we navigate through 2024, the digital commerce sector is witnessing its most significant transformation since the 2020 acceleration. This report identifies a shift from "convenience-first" to "experience-first" shopping models, driven by generative AI and hyper-personalization.

  • Market Growth Global e-commerce is projected to hit $6.8 trillion, a 12% YoY increase despite inflationary pressures.
  • AI Adoption 84% of leading retailers have integrated predictive AI into their supply chain management systems.
  • Mobile Dominance Mobile-first transactions now account for 74.2% of total digital sales in emerging markets.
  • Sustainability Circular economy features like resale and trade-in programs grew 3x faster than traditional retail.
